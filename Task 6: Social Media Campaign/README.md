# 📱 Task 06 – Social Media Campaign

## 📌 Objective

Design a cohesive social media campaign consisting of multiple posts that maintain a consistent brand identity, visual style, and messaging across all designs.

---

## 📝 Project Description

This project showcases a **4-post social media campaign** created for **Synent Technologies** using **Figma**. Each post follows a unified design system, including consistent colors, typography, layout, and branding elements to strengthen the company's online presence.

The campaign was designed to promote the company's services, engage the audience, and communicate key brand messages effectively.

---

## ✨ Campaign Includes

- Welcome Post
- Our Services Post
- Join Our Team (Hiring) Post
- Thank You Post

---

- Consistent Brand Identity
